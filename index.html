Non-diabetic Diabetic You

The dashed lines split risk into bands. The figure above each band is the share of people in that band who turned out to be diabetic — the honest probability for that range.

Important. This is a screening aid and a personal-project demonstration — not a medical device. It does not diagnose anyone and does not replace assessment by a qualified clinician.

Behind the scenes this runs a logistic-regression model trained by gradient descent on a public blood-work dataset; confirmed results fine-tune it further. The model, its training, and its limitations are written up in the project README and report. Everything runs locally in your browser.
